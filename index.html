About

Socializing your puppy at a young age is important for their development.

Meetup with other puppies in a safe and controlled environment, so that your puppy can learn and have fun at the same time.

A professional dog trainer will be on site to give advice and answer any questions in regards to your dog.

Meetups are free but limited spaces are available to ensure a controlled environment.
